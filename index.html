our services

24*7 Emergency Services

PalCare Center operates 24-hour emergency services. A team of Emergency Medicine doctors with highly skilled paramedics deal with cases from minor injuries to patients requiring advanced trauma care.

learn more

24*7 Critical Care Ambulance Service

The PalCare Ambulance aims to provide ‘medical services at your doorstep’. It has all the necessary equipments like a ventilator, defibrillator etc., with trained doctors and nurses so that the medical treatment can start right at the door step of critically ill patients.

learn more

Day Care Facility

Our eight bedded Day-Care centre caters to patients undergoing minor surgeries and procedures like chemotherapy, administration of intravenous drugs etc. that do not require the patients to stay more than a few hours or overnight stay in the hospital.

learn more

Intensive Care - Adult(ICU/HDU)

Our ICU is spacious with a very high area per bed as per international guidelines. Large windows allow plenty of natural lighting. It is equipped with the latest and modern Ventilators, Haemo-filtration equipments for renal complications, monitoring equipments for heart rates, blood pressure (invasive / non invasive) etc. There is an isolation bed for infected patients.

learn more

24*7 Pathology

Our pathology laboratory is functional 24 hours and is fully equipped to cover tests of Biochemistry & Hormones, Hematology, Cardiac Markers, Serology and Microbiology. The laboratory facility is also available for walk-in customers apart from the hospital’s IP & OPD patients. Reports are available on-line and can be retrieved from our website. Home collection of samples is also available with certain conditions.

learn more

Ultra Modern OT Complex

Our OT complex comprises of three OTs with laminar flow and hepa filtration, the latest motorized OT tables, OT lights, C-Arm imaging facility and modern Anaesthesia work station. There is an attached PACU.

learn more

about us

The best of modern healthcare, to ensure you stay healthy always

As country’s leading multispecialty health care facilities, Palcare center has a well-earned reputation of being the pioneer of healthcare in India. Established in 1983, Palcare has touched millions of lives, providing the best care and treatment for simple to most complicated diseases and conditions to the community. Combining the best available technology, medical expertise and equipment, we are redefining healthcare excellence to ensure all our patients receive comprehensive and integrated service with utmost compassion, support and care.
